Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Introduction

FlowTok Poster ("we", "our", "us") is a private content-scheduling tool operated by our ministry to publish our own short videos to our own official TikTok account. This Privacy Policy explains what information the application accesses and how it is used.

Information We Access

Through the TikTok API, and only for the TikTok account that explicitly authorizes the application, we access:

  • Basic account information (username and profile details) via the user.info.basic scope;
  • The ability to upload and publish videos to that account via the video.publish scope.

We do not collect, store, or process personal information from viewers, followers, or any third parties. The application only handles videos and captions that we ourselves own and choose to publish.

How We Use Information

The access granted through the TikTok API is used solely to:

  • Schedule and publish our own videos to our own authorized TikTok account;
  • Confirm the publishing status of those videos.

We do not use the access for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than publishing our own content.

Data Storage and Sharing

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to communicate with the TikTok API. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. The videos we publish are hosted temporarily on our own media account (Cloudinary) solely to deliver them to TikTok, and are removed after use.

Data Retention

We retain only the metadata required to operate the scheduling tool (our own video captions and publishing status). Authorization can be revoked at any time from your TikTok account settings, which immediately ends our access.

Your Rights

Because the application is operated by us for our own account, you may revoke its access at any time through TikTok's "Manage app permissions" settings. To request deletion of any stored data, contact us below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
