FlowTok Poster

A personal tool to schedule and automatically publish my own short videos and photo posts to my own TikTok account at set times.

Connect your TikTok account

Log in with TikTok to authorize FlowTok Poster to publish your scheduled posts.

Log in with TikTok

How it works

  1. Log in with TikTok (button above) to connect your account.
  2. Your videos and photo posts are queued in advance with their captions.
  3. At your chosen times, FlowTok Poster publishes each one to your account using the TikTok Content Posting API.

It only accesses your own authorized account and collects no data from anyone else.
