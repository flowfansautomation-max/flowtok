Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Introduction

FlowTok Poster ("the app", "I", "me") is a personal tool I built and use to publish my own short videos to my own TikTok account. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

Information the App Accesses

Through the TikTok API, and only for my own TikTok account which I personally authorize, the app accesses:

  • Basic account information (username and profile details) via the user.info.basic scope;
  • The ability to upload and publish videos to my account via the video.publish scope.

The app does not collect, store, or process personal information from viewers, followers, or any other people. It only handles videos and captions that I myself own and choose to publish.

How Information Is Used

The access granted through the TikTok API is used solely to:

  • Schedule and publish my own videos to my own authorized TikTok account;
  • Check the publishing status of those videos.

The access is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than publishing my own content.

Data Storage and Sharing

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to communicate with the TikTok API. No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties. The videos are hosted temporarily on my own media account (Cloudinary) solely to deliver them to TikTok, and are removed after use.

Data Retention

The app retains only the information needed to operate, namely my own video captions and their publishing status. I can revoke the app's access at any time from my TikTok account settings, which immediately ends its access.

Your Rights

Because the app is used by me for my own account, access can be revoked at any time through TikTok's "Manage app permissions" settings. To request deletion of any stored data, use the contact below.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
